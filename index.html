Sample Lecture 2: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating machines and software capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from data, recognizing patterns, understanding language, making decisions, and solving problems. AI powers many technologies we use today—such as voice assistants, smart cameras, recommendation systems, and self-driving cars—making our lives easier, faster, and more efficient.
